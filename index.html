  • Sravanam

    Systematic way of listening to the truth or studying the truth, At the end of this stage you must able to claim "I have understood what they want to say"

  • Mananam

    It is a thinking stage, After understanding what they want to say you may believe it or you may not, this is the stage where your acceptance level will reach 100% at the end by removing all your questions.

  • Nididhyasanam

    After mananam truth will completely be digested in you but you may not feel it, this nididhyasanam is the stage you will able to feel it by practice and by meditation, at end of nididhyasanam realization will attain this helps to transform our present beingness.
